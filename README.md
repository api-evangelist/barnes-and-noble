# Barnes & Noble (barnes-and-noble)
Barnes & Noble is the largest retail bookseller in the United States, operating physical bookstores and an e-commerce site selling books, magazines, gifts, and educational products. Barnes & Noble also operates the Nook e-reader platform and offers affiliate and publisher partner programs.
